# PART 4

# PLATFORM OWNER PORTAL COMPLETE SPECIFICATION

## Overview

The Platform Owner Portal is the master SaaS administration system.

This portal is exclusively for the platform owner and internal platform management team.

Purpose:

Manage:

* Companies
* Subscriptions
* Plans
* Users
* Revenue
* Tenant Activity
* System Health
* Audit Logs
* Platform Analytics

This portal must be completely separate from Customer Admin Portal.

Route:

```text
/platform
```

Only Platform Owner users can access.

Non-platform users must be automatically redirected.

---

# Platform Owner Portal Objectives

The Platform Owner must be able to:

* Create Companies
* Manage Companies
* Suspend Companies
* Activate Companies
* Manage Plans
* Manage Limits
* Manage Tenant Access
* Monitor Usage
* Monitor Growth
* View Revenue
* View Audits
* Manage Platform Settings
