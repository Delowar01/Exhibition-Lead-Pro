# PART 8

# MOBILE APP ARCHITECTURE, OFFLINE MODE, FIELD SALES EXPERIENCE, TEAM PERFORMANCE & GAMIFICATION

## Overview

The mobile application is the primary product of the platform.

Most users will spend 90% of their time inside the mobile application while attending exhibitions, conferences, trade shows, networking events, and client meetings.

The application must feel:

* Premium
* Fast
* Native
* Offline-first
* Enterprise-grade

The app should feel closer to Salesforce Mobile, HubSpot Mobile, and LinkedIn Premium than a traditional card scanner application.
