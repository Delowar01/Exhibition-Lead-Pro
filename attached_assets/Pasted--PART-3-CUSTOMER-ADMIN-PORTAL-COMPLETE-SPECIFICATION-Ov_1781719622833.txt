# PART 3

# CUSTOMER ADMIN PORTAL COMPLETE SPECIFICATION

## Overview

The Customer Admin Portal is the operational control center for each subscribing company.

Purpose:

Manage:

* Users
* Leads
* Duplicates
* Follow-Ups
* Events
* Team Performance
* ROI
* Reports
* Analytics
* Company Settings

Users:

* Primary Admin
* Admin
* Managers
* Authorized Employees (limited)

URL Example:

```text
/company
```
