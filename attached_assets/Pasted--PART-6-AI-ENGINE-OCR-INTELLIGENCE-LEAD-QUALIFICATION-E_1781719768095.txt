# PART 6

# AI ENGINE, OCR INTELLIGENCE, LEAD QUALIFICATION & ENRICHMENT SYSTEM

## Overview

This module transforms the platform from a simple lead collection system into a true AI-powered Exhibition Lead Intelligence Platform.

The AI Engine becomes the brain of the entire platform.

Every captured lead should be automatically analyzed, enriched, scored, qualified, summarized, categorized, and prioritized.

The AI system must support:

* Arabic
* English
* Bilingual GCC business cards
* Voice notes
* Event badges
* QR profiles
* Manual lead entries

---
