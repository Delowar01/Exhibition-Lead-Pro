# PART 9

# SECURITY, COMPLIANCE, SUBSCRIPTION BILLING, SAAS MANAGEMENT & MULTI-TENANT ARCHITECTURE

## Overview

This platform is designed as a multi-tenant SaaS solution.

The system must support:

* Single Company Deployment
* Multi-Company SaaS Deployment
* Enterprise Deployment
* Organizer Deployment
* White Label Deployment

All tenants must remain completely isolated.

No tenant must ever be able to access another tenant's data unless explicitly granted by the Platform Owner.

---

# SaaS Architecture

## Hierarchy

```text
Platform Owner

├── Company A
│    ├── Primary Admin
│    ├── Admin
│    └── Employees
│
├── Company B
│    ├── Primary Admin
│    ├── Admin
│    └── Employees
│
└── Company C
     ├── Primary Admin
     ├── Admin
     └── Employees
```

# User Hierarchy

## Level 1

Platform Owner

Full system access.

---

## Level 2

Primary Admin

Company owner.

Controls company users.

Can create admins.

Cannot access platform settings.

---

## Level 3

Admin

Department manager.

Can manage assigned users.

Cannot create other admins.

---

## Level 4

Employee

Standard user.

Limited permissions.
