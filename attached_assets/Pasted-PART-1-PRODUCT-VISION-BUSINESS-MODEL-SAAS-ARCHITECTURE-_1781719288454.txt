SaaS Tenant Architecture
The platform must be fully multi-tenant.
Data isolation is mandatory.
No company can access another company's data.
All records must contain:
tenant_idcompany_idcreated_bycreated_atupdated_at
All API endpoints must automatically filter by tenant.
