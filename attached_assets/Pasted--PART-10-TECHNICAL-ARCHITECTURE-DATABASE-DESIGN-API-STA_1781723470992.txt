## Overview

This document defines the engineering standards for the entire AI-Powered Exhibition Lead Capture Platform.

The platform must be built as a modern enterprise-grade SaaS solution capable of supporting:

* Mobile Applications
* Web Admin Portal
* Platform Owner Portal
* Organizer Portal
* APIs
* AI Services
* Workflow Engine
* Offline Synchronization

for thousands of companies and millions of leads.
