# PART 5

# ORGANIZER PORTAL & EVENT INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM

## Overview

The Organizer Portal is a completely separate enterprise-grade portal designed for:

* Exhibition Organizers
* Event Organizers
* Conference Organizers
* Trade Show Management Companies
* Expo Operators

Examples:

* LEAP
* GITEX
* Intersec
* Big 5
* Saudi Rail
* Saudi Plastics
* Saudi Food Manufacturing
* Future Projects Forum
* Cityscape
* Arab Health

This portal is NOT for exhibitors.

This portal provides event-wide intelligence while preserving exhibitor data privacy.

---

# Core Objective

Allow organizers to:

* Monitor event performance
* Measure exhibitor engagement
* Analyze visitor interactions
* View platform-wide event analytics
* Generate event reports
* Improve future event planning

WITHOUT exposing private exhibitor lead data.

* Mobile numbers
* Notes
* Voice recordings
