# PART 7

# WHATSAPP AUTOMATION, EMAIL AUTOMATION, WORKFLOW ENGINE, CRM INTEGRATIONS & AI PROPOSAL GENERATOR

## Overview

This module converts captured leads into actual business opportunities.

The platform must not stop at lead collection.

It must help users:

* Engage leads
* Automate follow-ups
* Generate proposals
* Schedule meetings
* Create opportunities
* Track conversions
* Close deals

The entire workflow should be optimized for exhibition sales teams operating in GCC markets.
