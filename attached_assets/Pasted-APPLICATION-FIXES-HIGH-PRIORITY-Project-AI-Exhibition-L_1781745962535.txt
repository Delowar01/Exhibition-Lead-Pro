1. DUPLICATE MANAGEMENT FIX (CRITICAL)

   ====================================================



Current Issue:



The system correctly detects duplicate leads.



However, duplicate leads are still appearing in the main Contacts list and are being included in counts and statistics.



This is incorrect.



Required Behaviour:



Contacts Screen:



Show ONLY unique/original contacts.



Example:



Lead A

Lead B

Lead C



If Lead A is scanned 10 times:



Contacts List:



✓ Lead A

✓ Lead B

✓ Lead C



Duplicates List:



✓ Lead A (Duplicate #1)

✓ Lead A (Duplicate #2)

✓ Lead A (Duplicate #3)

✓ Lead A (Duplicate #4)

✓ Lead A (Duplicate #5)

✓ Lead A (Duplicate #6)

✓ Lead A (Duplicate #7)

✓ Lead A (Duplicate #8)

✓ Lead A (Duplicate #9)



Rules:



* Original record remains in Contacts.

* All duplicate records move to Duplicates.

* Duplicate records must never appear in Contacts.

* Duplicate records must not be counted anywhere in the application except the Duplicates section.

* Dashboard statistics must use unique contacts only.

* Contact count must exclude duplicates.

* Reports must exclude duplicates.

* Analytics must exclude duplicates.

* Pipeline and status counts must exclude duplicates.

* Duplicate count must be shown separately.

* Duplicates must be stored only in the Duplicates section.



Validation Required:



* Scan the same card 10 times.

* Verify only 1 appears in Contacts.

* Verify remaining 9 appear in Duplicates.

* Verify dashboard counts remain unchanged after duplicate scans.

* Verify reports and analytics do not include duplicates.
